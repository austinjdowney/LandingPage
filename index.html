Austin Downey

Born in New York, raised in Idaho, and graduated from Pacific University in Forest Grove, Oregon with a B.S. in Psychology with a Neuroscience emphasis and a minor in Sociology. After moving back to the Boise area, I was introduced to software development where I completed the CodeWorks bootcamp in 13 weeks, and fell in love with being able to apply my psychology background and the instant gratification of seeing code come to life. I do prefer front-end development because of that immediate release of visually seeing the variety of ways I am able to tweak styling, but I am not definitely not opposed to developing a full-stack application from the ground up! Please feel free to browse through some of my past applications below. Stay tuned to find out some of the applications that are under way, as well!
